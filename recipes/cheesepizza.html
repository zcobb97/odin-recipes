Cheese-pizza

Description

a dish of Italian origin consisting of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese, typically with added meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheeses
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make pizza dough into flat circle
  2. Add tomato sauce, cheeses, and spices on the top of the dough
  3. Bake until dough is a golden brown
