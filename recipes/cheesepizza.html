Cheese-pizza

Description

a dish of Italian origin consisting of a flat, round base of dough baked with a topping of tomato sauce and cheese, typically with added meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

    Pizza dough Cheeses Tomato sauce Spices

Steps

    Make pizza dough into flat circle Add tomato sauce, cheeses, and spices on the top of the dough Bake until dough is a golden brown
